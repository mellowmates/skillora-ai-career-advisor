About Skillora AI

Skillora AI is a state-of-the-art career advisor platform designed to empower Indian students and professionals to make informed and personalized career decisions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible and AI-driven career guidance, combining proven psychological models, market intelligence, and machine learning, tailored to the Indian education and employment ecosystem.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Assessments: Personality, skill, and interest evaluations based on robust frameworks.
  • Hybrid Career Recommendations: A blend of rule-based and machine learning models ensures reliable and personalized guidance.
  • Real-Time Market Insights: Up-to-date data on job trends, salary benchmarks, and sector performance in India.
  • Learning Roadmaps: Customized skill development plans with resource recommendations.
  • Interactive AI Chatbot: 24/7 support for career queries and planning.

Our Team

Developed by a passionate group of students and AI enthusiasts participating in the 2025 Indian Hackathon, with contributions from career counselors and data scientists.
